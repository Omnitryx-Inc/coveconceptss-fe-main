Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CC-SHOP ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

Information We Collect

When you place an order or interact with our website, we may collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Shipping address (for physical orders)
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Browser and device information for analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process orders, send order confirmations and digital downloads, provide customer support, and improve our products and website experience. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Email Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter or make a purchase, we may send you emails about new products, promotions, and updates. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience, remember your cart contents, and analyze website traffic. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Payment processing is handled by secure third-party providers and we do not store payment card details on our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To make such a request, contact us through our Instagram or TikTok channels.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach us on Instagram.
