Exchange Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Physical Products

We accept exchanges on unused, unopened physical items within 14 days of delivery. To initiate an exchange, please contact us through our social channels with your order number and the reason for your request.

Digital Downloads

Due to the nature of digital products, all digital sales are final once the file has been downloaded or accessed. If you experience a technical issue with your download, we will happily resend your file or offer a replacement.

Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged, please photograph the damage and contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will send a replacement at no additional charge.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Digital downloads that have been accessed
  • Items that have been used or opened
  • Gift sets where packaging has been broken
  • Items purchased on sale or with a discount code

How to Request an Exchange

Reach out to us on Instagram with your order number. We aim to respond within 1-2 business days and will guide you through the process.

Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived damaged or was sent incorrectly. We recommend using a trackable shipping method for all returns.
