Shipping Info

Last updated: March 2026

Processing Time

All physical orders are processed within 2-5 business days. During peak seasons (Eid, Ramadan, holidays), processing may take up to 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your order has shipped.

Digital Downloads

Digital products are delivered instantly via email after your payment is confirmed. You will receive a secure download link within minutes of purchase. If you do not receive your email, please check your spam folder or contact us.

Delivery Estimates

  • Standard Shipping (US): 5-10 business days
  • Expedited Shipping (US): 2-4 business days
  • International: 10-21 business days (availability varies by country)

Free Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all US orders over $50. Free shipping is automatically applied at checkout when your order qualifies.

Tracking

A tracking number will be emailed to you once your order ships. You can use this to track your package through the carrier's website. Please allow 24-48 hours for tracking information to update.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged or does not arrive within the estimated timeframe, please contact us at the email listed in your order confirmation. We will work quickly to resolve the issue.

Contact Us

Have questions about your order? Reach out to us on Instagram or TikTok and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
